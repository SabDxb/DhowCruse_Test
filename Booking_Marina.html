Dhow Cruise Abu Dhabi Overview

Enjoy a memorable evening aboard a traditional Arabian boat ‘Dhow’ along the corniche. Meticulously crafted Arabian sailing vessels have become iconic on the waters of Abu Dhabi, and are enjoyed by both tourists and locals alike.

Our two-hour evening dinner dhow cruise offers you a unique chance to admire the inimitable craftsmanship of our traditional dhow with a distinct ambience reminiscent of the classic bygone era. The moment you step into the dhow, our friendly team will guide you to your table where you can make yourself comfortable for a classy dining cruise unlike any on land.

The buffet dinner is served as soon as the dhow sets off along the water. Choose your meal from a widespread and creative selection of delicious appetizers, entrees, and desserts. The menu fuses the finest haute with classic Arabian specialties to complement your culinary experience. Guests can also enjoy a range of snacks as well as unlimited refreshments while cruising. What’s even more striking about this cruise is that it provides you an extraordinary vantage point on water; simply head out to deck offering unobstructed magnificent views over the skyline of Abu Dhabi.

Altogether it’s an ideal way to enjoy a great evening with a romantic partner, entertain clients or out of town guests, or surprise your dear ones visiting from far. Whatever you wish – be it a scenic cruise or a memorable evening, we look forward to greet you onboard. Catering to discriminating travelers with a love for rich Arabian heritage and peaceful relaxation, this breathtaking cruise can be taken at any time of the year.

The average time to visit the Aquarium Tunnel is approximately 15 to 30 minutes. Underwater Zoo, located on Level 2, takes about 45 mins – 1.5 hours. This time could increase should you wish to stay and watch presentations/shows. The Glass Bottom Boat ride lasts approximately 15 – 20 minutes. Guests are encouraged to book their Boat Ride before their journey to avoid a long waiting period, and bookings can be made at the entrance of the Glass Bottom Boat ride area on Level 2 at Underwater Zoo.
